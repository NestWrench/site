NestWrench Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how NestWrench (a service of Davidson Enterprises, LLC, Crown Point, Indiana, USA) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile app, website, AI Scan features, and Live Video Consult features (the “Service”).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Account information: Name, email, password, and contact details you provide when creating an account.
  • Payment information: We work with trusted payment processors (for example, Stripe) to handle card data. We do not store full credit card numbers on our own servers.
  • Service usage: Actions you take in the app, such as creating a maintenance log or booking a consult.
  • Diagnostics content: Photos, video, and descriptions you upload for AI Scan or share with an expert during a Live Video Consult.
  • Device data: Basic technical details like IP address, device type, app version, and crash logs. We use these to secure and improve the Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the Service, including diagnostics, consults, reminders, and cost estimates.
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • Personalize guidance — for example, suggesting seasonal maintenance tasks.
  • Improve AI Scan accuracy and overall app performance.
  • Communicate with you about updates, billing, security alerts, or changes to these policies.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share limited information with:

  • Payment processors: To securely complete transactions (e.g., Stripe).
  • Independent professionals: If you request a Live Video Consult or local service, we may share relevant details (like the problem description, photos, or location you provide) so they can assist you.
  • Service providers: Vendors who help us host data, send emails, analyze performance, or detect fraud. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Law and safety: If required by law, regulation, subpoena, or to protect safety, property, or legal rights of you, us, other users, or the public.

We do not sell your personal information for advertising purposes.

4. Data Security

We use technical, administrative, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. No system is 100% secure, but we work to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of data. You can help by using a strong password and keeping it private.

5. Your Choices

  • Account access: You may view or update certain account info in your profile settings.
  • Subscription management: You may cancel recurring billing in the app under Account → Manage Subscription. Cancelling stops future renewals.
  • Diagnostic content: You can request that we delete specific uploaded photos or videos by emailing support@nestwrench.app. Please include enough detail to identify the content.
  • Marketing emails: You can opt out of non-essential marketing emails by using the unsubscribe link in those emails.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us information, contact support@nestwrench.app and we will address it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last Updated” date above and, where appropriate, notify you in-app or by email.

8. Contact

For questions about privacy, data usage, or data removal, contact us at support@nestwrench.app.
