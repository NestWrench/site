NestWrench Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how NestWrench, a service of Davidson Enterprises, LLC (Crown Point, Indiana, USA), collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile app and website (the “Service”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name, email, and password used to create your account.
  • Diagnostic content: Photos or videos you upload for AI Scan analysis.
  • Device data: IP address, device type, and app version for security and analytics.
  • Payment info: Stripe processes payments securely on our behalf. We never store full card details.

2. How We Use Data

  • Operate and improve the app and AI Scan performance.
  • Provide repair suggestions and organize your maintenance logs.
  • Send essential notifications (e.g., billing receipts or security alerts).
  • Analyze anonymized usage patterns to enhance the Service.

3. Sharing Information

We may share limited data only with:

  • Trusted vendors providing hosting, analytics, or payment processing (e.g., Stripe).
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to protect safety.

We do not sell personal data to advertisers.

4. Data Retention

Uploaded photos and diagnostics are stored securely and deleted upon your request or after account closure. You can contact support@nestwrench.app to request deletion.

5. Security

We use encryption, access controls, and secure storage to protect data. While no system is 100% secure, we take steps to reduce risks of unauthorized access.

6. Your Rights

  • Request a copy or deletion of your data by emailing support.
  • Cancel subscriptions or opt out of marketing messages anytime.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be posted here with a revised “Last Updated” date.
